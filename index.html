Welcome

If you were redirected to this page from https://wowsims.github.io, it's due to a recent update we made to try to launch the site on the wowsims.com domain.

We had to revert this change, but if you accessed the site while it was active you will have to clear some of your browser's cache data to access the sim from this browser.

We've provided instructions for Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome below.

Mozilla Firefox

Navigate to your Settings, then under Privacy & Security search for the Clear Data option.

From there, uncheck the box for Cookies and site data to avoid losing your saved logins and data, but keep Temporary cached files and pages checked.

Once you press Clear you should once again be able to access https://wowsims.github.io.
